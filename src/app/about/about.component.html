Departments

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with the latest skills and knowledge in computer science. Our state-of-the-art labs and experienced faculty provide a robust learning environment that fosters innovation and research.

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering focuses on cutting-edge technologies and practical applications. Students are trained in various aspects of electronics, communication systems, and signal processing, preparing them for successful careers in the industry.

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering provides a strong foundation in mechanical principles and hands-on experience in modern engineering practices. Our graduates are well-prepared to tackle challenges in manufacturing, design, and thermal sciences.

Department of Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering offers a dynamic curriculum that covers various aspects of civil engineering, including structural analysis, construction management, and environmental engineering. Our students are trained to become skilled professionals capable of managing complex infrastructure projects.

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering provides comprehensive training in electrical systems, electronics, and power engineering. Our graduates are equipped with the skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving field of electrical engineering.
